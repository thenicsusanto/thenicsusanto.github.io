About Me

I started with developing small projects on scratch, and then worked primarily using Python and Java. I have also experimented with many other languages such as Swift and C++, but I've moved into mainly using C# in Unity. I'm now learning HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

Additionally, I teach kids Unity, making my own curriculum, and have developed many prototypes and video games. Although I am practicing for USACO and learning other languages, my biggest passion has always been in game development.

Outside of coding, I am involved with various organizations: Model United Nations, Golf Team, Math Club, and Esports and Game Club as an executive board member.
